###
Disease: TB (Tuberculosis / Tap-e-Diq)
Symptoms: 2 haftay se ziada khansi, wazan kam hona, sham ke waqt bukhar, balgham mein khoon aana.
Short-term relief: Aaram karein, healthy khana khayen, mask pehan kar rakhein.
Treatment: TB ka mukammal course (ATT) jo 6 mahinay chalta hai. Beech mein dawai chorna khatarnak hai. Doctor se mashwara zaroori hai.
Precautionary measures: 
* Khantay waqt mun par kapra rakhein.
* Bachon ko paidaish ke baad BCG ka teeka lagwayen.
* Hawa daar kamray mein rahein.

###
Disease: Ghabrahat (Anxiety)
Symptoms: Dil ki dharkan tez hona, hath paon kanpna, bechaini, bila waja khauf mehsoos hona.
Short-term relief: Gehra saans lein (Deep breathing), paani piyein, apni tawajja kisi aur taraf lagayen.
Treatment: Therapy aur anti-anxiety medicines (jaise Alprazolam - sirf doctor ki parchi par). Doctor se mashwara zaroori hai.
Precautionary measures: 
* Caffeine (chai/coffee) ka istemal kam karein.
* Rozana warzish (exercise) karein.
* Neend poori karein.

###
Disease: Sar ki Juyein (Head Lice)
Symptoms: Sar mein har waqt kharish hona, khaas tor par kaan ke peeche aur gardan par.
Short-term relief: Bareek kanghi (lice comb) ka istemal karein.
Treatment: Anti-lice shampoo/lotion (Coopex waghaira). Doctor se mashwara zaroori hai.
Precautionary measures: 
* Doosron ki kanghi, topi, ya takiya istemal na karein.
* Bachon ke sar ki safayi ka khayal rakhein.
* Bistar ki Chadar garam paani se dhoyen.

###
Disease: Safed Motia (Cataract)
Symptoms: Nazar dhundla jana, raat ko dekhnay mein dushwari, roshni ke gird dairay (halos) nazar aana.
Short-term relief: Tez roshni se bachein, chashma lagayen.
Treatment: Iska wahid hal chota sa operation (Surgery) hai jis mein lens tabdeel hota hai. Doctor se mashwara zaroori hai.
Precautionary measures: 
* Dhoop mein kala chashma pehnein.
* Sugar (Diabetes) ko control mein rakhein.
* Smoking se parhez karein.

###
Disease: Chakkar Aana (Vertigo)
Symptoms: Aisa lagna ke kamra ghoom raha hai, sar ghoomna, ulti aana, tawazan (balance) kharab hona.
Short-term relief: Foran zameen par lait jayen, aankhein band kar lein, sar ko tezi se na hilayen.
Treatment: Anti-vertigo medicines (jaise Serc / Betahistine). Doctor se mashwara zaroori hai.
Precautionary measures: 
* Achanak kharay hone se guraiz karein.
* Sar ke neeche do takiye (pillows) rakh kar soyen.
* Stress kam lein.

###
Disease: Mahwari ka dard (Menstrual Cramps)
Symptoms: Pait ke nichlay hissay mein dard, kamar dard, taango mein dard, chichrapan.
Short-term relief: Garam paani ki bottle (Hot water bottle) pait par rakhein, adrak ki chaye piyein.
Treatment: Painkillers (jaise Ponstan ya Brufen) aur Antispasmodics (Buscopan). Doctor se mashwara zaroori hai.
Precautionary measures: 
* Inn dino mein thandi cheezon se parhez karein.
* Halka phulka chaltay phirtay rahein (walk karein).
* Pani ziada piyein.

###
Disease: Tetanus (Tashanuj / Chandi)
Symptoms: Jabra (jaw) lock hona, gardan aur pathon mein akrao, nigalnay mein dushwari.
Short-term relief: Foran hospital jayen, ye jaan lewa ho sakta hai.
Treatment: Tetanus Immunoglobulin aur Antibiotics. Hospital mein dakhla zaroori hai. Doctor se mashwara zaroori hai.
Precautionary measures: 
* Agar koi lohay ki cheez ya sadak par chot lagay to 24 ghantay mein Tetanus ka teeka (ATS) lagwayen.
* Bachpan mein vaccination poori karwayen.

###
Disease: Dhoop se jalna (Sunburn)
Symptoms: Jild ka laal hona, jalan, kharish, haath lagane par dard hona.
Short-term relief: Thanda paani dalein, Aloe Vera gel lagayen, dhoop mein jana band karein.
Treatment: Moisturizing creams aur dard ke liye Ibuprofen. Doctor se mashwara zaroori hai.
Precautionary measures: 
* Dhoop mein nikalnay se pehle Sunblock lagayen.
* Poori aasteen ke kapray pehnein.
* Subah 10 bajay se dopehar 4 bajay tak dhoop se bachein.
